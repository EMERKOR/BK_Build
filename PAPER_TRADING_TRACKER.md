## Strategy Reminder

### What We're Betting
- **Only favorites** when model has ‚â•5 point edge
- Model must say "favorite should win by MORE than Vegas thinks"
- Typical volume: 1-2 bets per week

### Why It Works (Theory)
- Vegas overadjusts favorites due to public betting
- Creates underpriced favorites
- Model identifies these specific cases

### Validated Performance (2020-2024)
- 306 bets total
- 54.6% win rate
- +4.6% ROI
- Consistent across 4 years

### Risk Factors
1. **Small edge**: Only 2.2% above vig threshold
2. **Variance**: Can lose 5-10 bets in a row by chance
3. **Market evolution**: Vegas could catch on and adjust
4. **Sample size**: 306 bets is good but not definitive
